About

Architects are critical members of the building team, because they are responsible for developing the fundamental design, look, and feel of a structure. In many regions, architectural drawings must be filed with permit applications, making the architect's services very necessary for people who want to do work legally. Architects can also be consulted over the course of a job as conditions change and adjustments need to be made to the original design. Work at an architecture firm can be quite varied, although many employees develop their own areas of expertise, such as renovations, single family residences, or secured facilities. A spirit of cooperation is often vitally important, as architects need to work with lots of different people in the course of their work, and they must also be able to communicate clearly, and to easily interpret communications from other people. Lost information in the planning stages can result in costly mistakes, and a good architecture firm should be able to prevent such problems with a skilled team of employees.
